Our Mission

Charity: water is a nonprofit organization bringing clean and safe drinking water to people in developing countries. Our mission is simple: solve the global water crisis in our lifetime. Every project we fund is built to last, and every dollar you give is invested directly into clean water solutions that transform lives, create healthier communities, and empower the future. Together, we can make access to clean water a reality for everyone, everywhere.
